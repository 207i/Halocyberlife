Overview

The Ocean Generator™ v2 is a modular near‑shore solar‑thermal seawater evaporation and condensation system engineered to deliver scalable freshwater production with minimal environmental impact.

Key Modules & Components

Seawater Intake Module

Passive screened intake with low‑pressure ecological protection.

Solar‑Thermal Evaporation

Segmented concentrators with fail‑safe shutters and thermal balancing.

Condensation Module

Heat exchange condensation and sealed freshwater collection.

Thermal Control & Safety

Shutters, sensors, and passive safety ensure safe operation under all conditions.

Modular Platform

Floating or fixed base with corrosion resistance and rapid deployment design.

Control & Automation

Monitoring of temperature, pressure, and system health with automated responses.

Isolation Architecture

Sealed conduit architecture protects electrical systems and ensures predictable failure behavior.
