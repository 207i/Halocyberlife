BioLoop™ Micro‑Biogas Capture: From Flush to Fuel

A complete overview of the patent‑pending BioLoop™ system — how it captures, separates, and stores micro‑biogas from toilet rim intake through advanced filtration and routing.

Core idea: odor & gas capture Goal: methane & CO₂ separation Systems: intake — filtration — storage

Patent Figures: Illustration suite corresponding to BioLoop™ patent diagrams.

The BioLoop™ Micro‑Biogas Capture System is an integrated hardware solution that captures airborne biogas directly at the toilet rim, routes it through engineered conduits, separates moisture and unwanted compounds, and ultimately stores clean gas for energy or analysis.

Through a series of purpose‑built components — from micro‑vent intakes to lung separation cores, manifolds, and enclosure modules — the system demonstrates how novel fluid dynamics and material layers can transform everyday waste emissions into controlled, usable streams.

BioLoop™ Features & Capabilities

Each component in the BioLoop™ platform contributes to a high‑performance biogas capture and filtration chain — adaptable from single‑bathroom use to facility‑wide installations.
