BioLoop™ Micro‑Biogas Capture: From Flush to Fuel

An in‑depth look at BioLoop™ — a patent‑pending system designed to capture, separate, and store micro‑biogas from toilet emissions using innovative intake and filtration technologies.

View Official BioLoop™ Patent ↗
Core idea: odor capture & gas separation Goal: methane & CO₂ purification System: intake — filtration — storage

Patent Figures: Visual summaries of core BioLoop™ components.

BioLoop™ captures airborne biogas as it exits a toilet through dedicated micro‑vents in the seat rim. Gas is routed via a sealed conduit to engineered separation chambers that remove moisture and odorous compounds while directing clean gas streams for processing and storage.

The system combines fluid dynamics, sorbent media, and sensor‑driven routing logic to isolate methane (CH₄), carbon dioxide (CO₂), and hydrogen sulfide (H₂S) into separate paths, enabling efficient capture and potential utilization.
