BioLoop™ Micro‑Biogas Capture: From Flush to Filtration

An overview of BioLoop™ — the patent‑pending system for toilet rim biogas capture, airflow routing, and multi‑stage filtration.

Core idea: odor & gas capture Goal: methane & CO₂ separation Systems: intake — filtration — storage

Patent Figures: High‑level illustrations corresponding to key BioLoop™ system components.

The BioLoop™ Micro‑Biogas Capture System captures airborne biogas emissions from toilet use, routes them through engineered conduits, separates unwanted moisture and compounds, and collects purified gases for storage or utilization.

From the micro‑vent toilet seat intake to the layered filtration core, back‑end manifold, and exterior module — BioLoop™ integrates fluid dynamics, material science, and real‑world installations into a complete clean‑tech solution.

BioLoop™ Features & Capabilities

Each component within the BioLoop™ platform contributes to high‑performance micro‑biogas capture, filtration, and routing — from single fixtures to facility‑scale deployments.
